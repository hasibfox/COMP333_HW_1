About Us!

Music Player Pro is platform where you can add music to your personalized playlists and then share it to the world! We allow people to create reviews, write rating from a scale from 1 to 5, and import new songs that they believe should be expressed to the world!

Our team uses state of the art algorithms to report proper playlist suggests and new songs based on the user profile information. For our techniques, we have received multiple awards that range from being amongst the Top 100 Tech Starts of 2022s to Top 25 Entertainment Software in US.

We offer our products in over 200 different language and have customer support and technical management all over the world 24/7. If you have any question or concerns about your account or would like to see new features to the platform, please reach out to us here!
